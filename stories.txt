As a user of Instagram can you do the following things:
1. You can create your user account.
2. You can log in and log out from your account. 
3. You can follow other people. 
4. Other people can follow you.
5. If you follow some person then you can see their posts, comments, 
   photos and videos uploaded by that person and vice-versa.
6. You can like others' photos and videos.
7. You can upload your post.
8. You can see all the people you follow.
9. You can see all the people that follow you.
10. you can update your post.
11. You can delete your post.
12. you can delete your account.
